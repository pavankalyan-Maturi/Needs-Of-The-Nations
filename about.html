About Us

Needs of the Nations is an organisation which aims to redistribute excess food from Restaurants,Hostels,Hotels, events to those who are hungry.

Hunger and Waste food shouldn't be at same place, it's our responsibility to take care , as our part of the duty we started this 'Needs of the Nations' to vanish those disparities We are in contact with hotels, restaurants and catering contractors so that whenever there is an Excess Food they easily inform us , information is sent via call or by website and then plan the transportation as according to the then day conditions We have similar contacts with orphanages and needy shelters or homes for which we provide this Excess Food before it losses it hygiene nature , we hardly store food unless it's something like fruit or vegetable. In this way we serve to them We need all of you to register in our website and increase our contact's both in areas of Excess Food and in areas that include Hunger, so that we strive hard to elimanate them along with our team of volunteers

Our Vision

  • To bring a social change in every individual in-order to reduce food waste and to make the World Hunger Free.

Our Mission

  • To feed the needy and hungry with untouchable edible surplus food.
  • To raise awareness about food loss and food waste amongst citizens and bring about behavioral change in them to prevent food waste at home, school or at workplace through guidance and sharing of good practices
